COMPUTER SCIENCE AND ENGNEERING

Computer Science Engineering is a course that deals with the design, implementation,
and management of information systems of both software & hardware processes.
A computer scientist specializes in the theory of computation and the design of
computational systems. Computer Science engineering aids with various disciplines
such as electrical and electronics engineering,information technology, software
engineering, and more.
